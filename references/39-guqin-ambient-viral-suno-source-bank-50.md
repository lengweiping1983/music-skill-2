# 古琴旧物氛围人声与 Suno 制作 50 条资料库

本资料库只用于支撑 `39-guqin-ambient-viral-suno-playbook.md` 的内部规则，不写入最终 `suno.md`。

## 古琴、中国传统音乐与旧音审美

1. 李晓梦《论中国琴学中的“韵”》，中国音乐，2026。要点：古琴“韵”是过程音、远虚松轻缓和有无之间的延展。https://cmj.ccmusic.edu.cn/zh/article/doi/10.13812/j.cnki.cn11-1379/j.2026.01.013/
2. Barry Yuk Bun Wan, “Application of the Aesthetic of Guqin for Contemporary Music Composition”, Revista Vortex, 2021。要点：古琴重视无严格拍号、单音色彩变化、泛音/按音/散音和装饰音。https://doaj.org/article/f8e3b0403c4b43899395fac220d8f977
3. Man Ding, “Harmony of Heaven, Earth and Human: An Aesthetic Anthropological Analysis of the Guqin’s Timbres”, 2024。要点：散音、按音、泛音与身体手势、文化身份相关。https://press.jandoo.ac/journal/csm/article/view/59
4. Zhihan Liu, “The Artistic Characteristics of Chinese Guqin Songs”, Art and Society, 2022。要点：古琴歌的调式、旋律、节奏、结构和歌词内容共同构成诗乐一体。https://www.paradigmpress.org/as/article/view/267
5. PMC, “Plucking Heartstrings: Guqin, Aesthetic Attunement, and TCM Music Therapy”, 2024。要点：古琴听感与身心调适、心弦隐喻、审美调谐有关。https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC13044482/
6. DOAJ/Revista Vortex 古琴当代作曲条目。要点：古琴哲学可转化为当代作曲技法，而不是只取样音色。https://doaj.org/article/f8e3b0403c4b43899395fac220d8f977
7. Music Theory Online, “Pentatonic Xuangong Transformations”, 2024。要点：中国五声音阶与旋宫转换是传统与当代分析的重要结构。https://mtosmt.org/issues/mto.24.30.1/mto.24.30.1.lam.html
8. Tandfonline, “Common and distinct quantitative characteristics of Chinese and Western music…”, 2024。要点：中国传统音乐的调式、音阶和旋律变体可量化比较。https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/09298215.2024.2311650
9. PMC, “Sound-color cross-modal correspondences in the Chinese pentatonic modes played on traditional instruments”, 2025。要点：传统乐器与五声音阶存在跨感官色彩联想，可用于画面化提示。https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12612230/
10. ISMIR, “Joint Analysis of Mode and Playing Technique in Guqin”, 2020。要点：古琴音乐的调式与演奏技法需要联合理解。https://archives.ismir.net/ismir2020/paper/000157.pdf
11. Canadian Acoustics, “Analysis and synthesis of the guqin”, 2003。要点：拨弦位置和声学建模会显著影响古琴音色。https://jcaa.caa-aca.ca/index.php/jcaa/article/view/1658
12. University of Minnesota, “Sonic fragility in compositions for guqin”。要点：破碎、分散和脆弱性可以成为古琴当代创作核心。https://conservancy.umn.edu/items/73d9e335-ed04-412a-8f10-8c66377e6857
13. New York Qin Society, “Composition as a Form of Musical Commentary in Guqin Tradition”, 2024。要点：古琴创作可理解为对传统的评论和再书写。https://newyorkqin.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/Ribeiro.-Composition-as-a-Form-of-Musical-Commentary-in-Guqin-Tradition.-Nov-2024..pdf
14. 艺术人类学《唐代琴意象中的古琴审美》。要点：幽静处、简静指法、琴心、琴德和味外疏淡是唐代琴意象的重要审美。https://www.artanthropology.com/show.aspx?cid=11&id=973
15. 《溪山琴况》“和淡清雅”相关论文条目。要点：和、淡、清、雅可转化为克制编曲和短混响尾。https://www.xueweilunwen.com/doc/433166
16. Wikipedia, Guqin。要点：断纹、丝弦、左手滑音和古琴历史可作为物件与声音细节参考。https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guqin
17. Wikipedia, Guqin aesthetics。要点：同音不同弦位产生不同音色，适合写“同一音的不同记忆”。https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guqin_aesthetics
18. Wikipedia, Guqin notation。要点：减字谱记录指法和位置而非单纯音高，提示歌词可写“手势”。https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guqin_notation
19. Wikipedia, Guqin tunings。要点：宫商角徵羽与调弦传统提醒不要把中国风简单写成西方大小调。https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guqin_tunings
20. ChinaAlbums, “The Chinese Pentatonic Scale”。要点：五声音阶各调式有不同情绪角色，可用于 Suno Style 的调性感描述。https://chinaalbums.com/chinese-music-pentatonic-scale

## 环境音乐、编曲、人声与混音

21. Transmit Samples, “Ambient Music Production”。要点：环境音乐依靠和声、空间、纹理、调制和留白。https://www.transmissionsamples.com/ambient-music-production
22. Magnetic Magazine, “Ambient Vocal Production”。要点：环境人声要给空间，少堆元素，逐渐铺垫再让人声成为焦点。https://magneticmag.com/2022/07/ambient-vocal-production/
23. Sonarworks, “Creating Ethereal Vocal Textures for Ambient and Downtempo Music”。要点：频率位置、动态移动和流动 timing 决定空灵人声。https://www.sonarworks.com/blog/learn/creating-ethereal-vocal-textures-for-ambient-and-downtempo-music
24. MusicProductionWiki, “Reverb”。要点：混响作为编曲空间，需要发送、EQ、自动化和全混音上下文判断。https://musicproductionwiki.com/bible/reverb
25. Beat Kitchen, “Reverb & Space”。要点：混响可以像乐队成员一样承担角色，但要被 EQ 和编曲服务。https://beatkitchen.io/guides/mixing-masterclass/07-reverb-and-space/
26. Producer Hub, “How to Use Reverb”。要点：人声要保持真实和靠前，混响需预延迟和高通避免糊。https://producerhub.co.uk/blog/how-to-use-reverb.html
27. Beat Kitchen, “Mixing Vocals”。要点：人声是多数歌曲的核心，制作要服务歌词、情绪和发音。https://beatkitchen.io/guides/mixing-masterclass/14-mixing-vocals/
28. RoastYourMix, “How to Mix Vocals”。要点：2-5kHz 影响清晰度，混响过多会把人声推后。https://roastyourmix.com/mix/vocals
29. Sage Audio, “How to Make Clear Vocals”。要点：人声清晰度来自中高频与低中频的关系，以及编曲让位。https://www.sageaudio.com/articles/how-to-make-clear-vocals-start-to-finish
30. DIY Music Guide, “Placing Vocals In The Mix”。要点：预延迟和 presence zone 可以帮助人声保持前景。https://www.diy-music-guide.com/articles/placing-vocals-in-the-mix
31. BCHillMix, “Vocal EQ Cheatsheet”。要点：人声前置和歌词快速理解依赖 presence 与空气感的克制处理。https://bchillmix.com/pages/vocal-eq-cheatsheet
32. MusicRadar, “Escape the 4-bar loop trap”。要点：尽早安排结构、每段改变能量、明确 hook 焦点。https://www.musicradar.com/tutorials/music-theory-songwriting/want-to-finally-finish-that-track-heres-how-to-escape-the-4-bar-loop-trap-and-actually-make-some-music
33. Dean Tellone, “How to Make Ambient Music”。要点：环境音乐也需要结构、层次和声音发展。https://deantellone.org/how-to-make-ambient-music-a-step-by-step-guide/
34. arXiv, “Instrumentational complexity of music genres and why simplicity sells”。要点：流行传播常与可识别、不过度复杂的编制有关。https://arxiv.org/abs/1405.5057
35. arXiv, “The Role of Communication and Reference Songs in the Mixing Process”。要点：制作意图需要被清楚传达，参考曲和语言会影响混音决策。https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.03404
