# 去 AI 味与真人感歌词 20篇专业来源清单

本清单服务于 `37-humanized-lyrics-playbook.md`。来源用于内部提炼，不得写入最终 `suno.md`。本 Skill 不做资料综述，只把资料压缩为可执行的歌词审稿规则。

## 20篇来源范围

1. Berklee Online: Prosody in Music and Songwriting。强调词义、语气、旋律重音和情绪方向必须一致。
   - https://online.berklee.edu/takenote/prosody-in-music-and-songwriting/
2. Berklee: Using Anaphora to Create Memorable Lyrics。强调重复句式要服务记忆和情绪递进，不能机械复制。
   - https://college.berklee.edu/bt/news/using-anaphora-to-create-memorable-lyrics
3. Berklee Online: Songwriting Tools and Techniques。强调歌词、旋律、和声、结构共同完成表达。
   - https://online.berklee.edu/courses/songwriting-tools-and-techniques
4. American Songwriter: Should I Always Have Furniture in My Lyrics? 强调具体物件能让歌词落地，但物件必须与情绪有关。
   - https://americansongwriter.com/should-i-always-have-furniture-in-my-lyrics/
5. TAXI: How Can I Avoid Cliches in My Lyrics? 强调避开陈词滥调，用个人化角度和具体说法替代熟套表达。
   - https://www.taxi.com/music-business-faq/songwriting/lyrics/cliches/
6. Soundfly: Songwriting for Producers: Three Ways to Introduce the Hook。强调 Hook 要早出现，并用编曲动作强化记忆。
   - https://flypaper.soundfly.com/tips/songwriting-for-producers-three-ways-to-introduce-the-hook/
7. Soundfly: How to Write Lyrical Hooks That Stick。强调 Hook 必须短、可复述、能被普通听众立刻抓住。
   - https://flypaper.soundfly.com/write/write-lyrical-hooks/
8. Soundfly: Better Lyric Writing。强调删掉空泛表达，让细节、动作和语气承担情绪。
   - https://flypaper.soundfly.com/tips/better-lyric-writing/
9. Soundfly: Chorus Writing Strategy 101。强调副歌要聚焦主情绪，避免把主题解释成作文。
   - https://flypaper.soundfly.com/write/chorus-writing-strategy-101/
10. ReverbNation: 4 Lyrical Cliches to Avoid。强调避开过熟短语、抽象大词和没有新角度的爱情套话。
   - https://blog.reverbnation.com/4-lyrical-cliches-avoid/
11. ReverbNation: Five Lyric Writing Tips for New Songwriters。强调保持自然表达、具体画面和可唱语流。
   - https://blog.reverbnation.com/five-lyric-writing-tips-for-new-songwriters/
12. Songtown: Poetic vs Conversational Song Lyrics。强调歌词要在诗意和口语之间取得平衡，不能只像漂亮句子。
   - https://songtown.com/on-songwriting/poetic-vs-conversational-song-lyrics/
13. Purdue OWL: Conciseness。强调删掉冗余、空泛修饰和重复解释。
   - https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/general_writing/academic_writing/conciseness/index.html
14. Purdue OWL: Plain Style。强调清楚、直接、读者容易理解的句子更有力量。
   - https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/general_writing/writing_style/plain_style%20.html
15. Montana State University Writing Center: Clear Language。强调具体、主动、少术语、少抽象化。
   - https://www.montana.edu/writingcenter/writing_resources/clearlanguage.html
16. UTEP University Writing Center: Cliches。强调陈词滥调会削弱原创性和可信度。
   - https://www.utep.edu/uwc/writing%20help%20online/cliches.html
17. Microsoft Copilot: Humanize AI Text。强调根据目的、读者和真实语境调整语气，避免机器式通用表达。
   - https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-copilot/copilot-101/humanize-ai-text
18. Grammarly: Humanizer User Guide。强调把 AI 生成内容改得更自然、符合语境和目标读者。
   - https://support.grammarly.com/hc/en-us/articles/38552339652109-Humanizer-user-guide
19. 宝玉：如何去掉 AI 写作味。强调从词汇、句式、结构和真实细节层面移除机器式套话。
   - https://baoyu.io/blog/2026-02-14-remove-ai-writing-flavor
20. Jack Righteous: Momentum Flow Control / Fix Stiff AI Lyrics。强调 AI 歌词常见问题是僵硬、过度解释和缺少演唱流动。
   - https://jackrighteous.com/blogs/music-creation-process-guide/momentum-flow-control-fix-stiff-ai-lyrics

## 使用纪律

- 来源只用于内部判断和 reference 文件，不得写入 `suno.md`。
- 不把“去 AI 味”理解为随便加口语词；必须落到场景、动作、语气、呼吸和可唱性。
- 发生冲突时，优先选择：像真人会唱出口、短句可唱、情绪有具体触发点、Hook 可被普通听众复述。
