# 好音乐本体质量 30篇专业来源清单

本清单服务于 `16-27` 号“好音乐本体质量”分项。来源用于内部提炼，不得写入 `suno.md`。本 Skill 不做论文综述，只把资料压缩为可执行的 Suno 写歌规则。

## 30篇来源范围

1. Berklee Online: Songwriting: Melody。
2. Berklee Online Take Note: What is Melody in a Song?。
3. Berklee Online Take Note: Writing Melodies: 3 Simple Tools。
4. Berklee Online Take Note: Prosody in Music and Songwriting。
5. Berklee Online: Songwriting Tools and Techniques。
6. Berklee Online: Songwriting: Writing Hit Songs。
7. Berklee: Using Anaphora to Create Memorable Lyrics。
8. Pat Pattison / Berklee lyric writing and prosody materials。
9. Jack Perricone / Berklee Press melody songwriting concepts。
10. Sheila Davis / lyric writing form, rhyme and structure concepts。
11. Hit Songs Deconstructed: melody, hook, chorus and structure analysis。
12. Soundfly: hook, melody, lyric and arrangement craft articles。
13. Songwriting.net: hook, rhyme, chorus and commercial writing practice。
14. Songwriting Authority: melody writing techniques and phrase contour。
15. Music Theory Authority: melodic contour and phrase structure。
16. ReLyMe: lyric-to-melody relationship research。
17. Research on lyric setting and natural language prosody。
18. Juslin / music emotion and expression research。
19. Quinto, Thompson and Taylor: composition/performance expression and emotion communication。
20. Beveridge and Knox: vocal melody and perceived emotion in popular music。
21. GEMS / Geneva Emotional Music Scale research。
22. Margulis: repetition and music cognition。
23. Huron: melodic expectation and musical anticipation。
24. Studies on groove, entrainment and rhythm perception。
25. Studies on music-evoked visual imagery and cross-modal association。
26. Janata / autobiographical memory and music。
27. Research on harmony, chord color and perceived emotional valence。
28. Production guidance from iZotope / LANDR / SoundOn / mastering engineers。
29. Vocal performance and timbre research, including formant/emotional perception。
30. Chinese lyric craft: 十三辙、意象、押韵、口语化叙事、Suno 中文发音稳定性。

## 使用纪律

- 每个分项都把30源结论转成“写歌怎么做”。
- 不在 `suno.md` 输出来源、理论、评分或分析。
- 发生冲突时，优先选择：好唱、好懂、好记、情绪真实、画面清楚、Suno 可执行。
